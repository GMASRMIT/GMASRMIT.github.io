About

Hello its GMAS!
We’re an anime club where all are welcome. Any uni, anywhere, member or not.

Activities
Come to our Friday Meetups, where we have Anime, Quizzes, Pictionary, Charades, Smash, Mario Kart and Prizes.

Big Events
